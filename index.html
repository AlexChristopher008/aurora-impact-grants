Aurora Impact Grants Ltd. supports entrepreneurs, students, non-profit organizations, and community initiatives through transparent and impact-driven grant programs.

About Us

Aurora Impact Grants Ltd. is an independent development-focused organization dedicated to promoting education, entrepreneurship, community development, and social innovation.

We collaborate with partners and funding stakeholders to support initiatives that create measurable social and economic impact.

Mission: To empower individuals and organizations with resources that enable positive and sustainable change.

Vision: A world where innovative ideas have fair access to opportunity.

Grant Programs

Small Business & Startup Grants

Support for early-stage businesses with scalable and sustainable ideas.

Education & Student Support

Funding assistance for education, skills development, and research initiatives.

Community Development

Support for projects addressing local social and economic challenges.

Women & Youth Empowerment

Focused funding for women-led and youth-driven initiatives.

Application Process

  1. Online application submission during open funding cycles.
  2. Eligibility and mission-alignment review.
  3. Evaluation and due diligence.
  4. Formal funding decision and notification.

Note: Aurora Impact Grants Ltd. does not guarantee funding and does not charge mandatory application fees.
